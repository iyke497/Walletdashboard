Deposits FAQ

Bloxxxchain does not charge any deposit fees.

Deposits require 1-3 block confirmations based on the coin/token.

If your deposit is not reflected in your Wallet, make sure that your destination address is correct and the deposit has received sufficient confirmations (1-3) on the blockchain.

If you require any assistance don't hesitate to contact our support team via email at support@bloxxxchain.com, WhatsApp or 24/7 live chat and provide us with the details of your transaction, we'll make sure to look into it.
